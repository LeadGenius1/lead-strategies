## ðŸ“§ Email Campaigns

### Create an Email Campaign

1. Go to "**Email Campaigns**" tab
2. Click "**New Email Campaign**"
3. Choose template or create custom
4. Write your email:
   - Subject line
   - Email body
   - Call-to-action
5. Select leads to email
6. Schedule or send immediately

### Email Best Practices

**Subject Lines:**
- Keep under 50 characters
- Personalize with {{name}} or {{company}}
- Avoid spam triggers (FREE, !!!, $$$)

**Email Body:**
- Start with personalization
- Clearly state value proposition
- Include one clear call-to-action
- Keep it concise (< 200 words)

**Timing:**
- Best days: Tuesday-Thursday
- Best times: 10 AM - 2 PM (recipient's timezone)
- Avoid Mondays and Fridays

## ðŸ”§ Troubleshooting

### "No leads found for my criteria"

**Solution:**
- Broaden your targeting criteria
- Try different industries or locations
- Check spelling of company names
- Verify location names are correct

### "Email bounce rate is high"

**Solution:**
- Use email verification tools
- Remove old/invalid emails
- Avoid free email providers
- Check spam score of your emails

### "Low open rates"

**Solution:**
- Improve subject lines (test multiple)
- Send at optimal times
- Verify sender reputation
- Personalize content

### "Can't export leads"

**Solution:**
- Check you haven't exceeded plan limit
- Try smaller export batch
- Clear browser cache
- Contact support if persists
